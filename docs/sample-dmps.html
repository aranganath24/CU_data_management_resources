Navigating the NIH Data Management and Sharing Policy (DMSP): Tools for CU Researchers

4 Sample DMPs

As noted above, DMPTool provides a catalog of DMPs written and used by researchers as part of their grant applications. This searchable archive of previously used DMPs provides additional examples that you may wish to explore. Another useful resource is this annotated example of a DMP in the provisional NIH 2023 format, which has been released by the NIH DMSP Guidance Working Group.

Perusing previously-submitted DMPs will give you a sense for the level of detail that reviewers expect in these documents.
